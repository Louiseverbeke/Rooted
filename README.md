Rooted

Overview

Rooted is a cultural community app designed for people living abroad, especially international students and young professionals. It helps users connect with others who share their language, culture, and background through profiles, friend requests, messaging, communities, and events. The goal of the app is to create a space for belonging, support, and cultural connection for people who are far from home.

About the Project

Rooted was created as my final project for my Intro to iOS Programming course while I was studying abroad at Boston College from King’s College London.

The idea for the app came from my own experience of living abroad and realizing how difficult it can be to find people who share your culture, language, and sense of home in a new environment. I wanted to build something that goes beyond a typical social platform and instead focuses on helping people abroad find friendship, support, community, and meaningful connections.

Key Technologies Used

- `SwiftUI` for the user interface and app structure
- `Firebase Authentication` for account creation, login, and user sessions
- `Cloud Firestore` for storing user profiles, communities, events, friend requests, and messages
- `Firebase Security Rules` for secure access control
- `UIKit` for profile image handling alongside SwiftUI

Core Features

- User sign up and login
- Personalized user profiles
- Friend requests, cancellation, acceptance, and removal
- Direct messaging between users
- Community creation and membership
- Community feeds for posting updates
- Community admin roles and management
- Event creation and RSVP functionality
- Personalized home screen with joined communities and upcoming RSVP’d events
- Past events section for RSVP’d events that have already taken place

Future Plans

In the future, I would like to continue developing Rooted beyond this course project. My long-term vision is to collaborate with universities and cultural societies around the world so the app can become a hub for people abroad who want to connect with others from their culture and discover relevant communities and events.

I would also like to eventually allow communities and societies to promote events directly through the app and later introduce ticket sales, with the app taking a small commission once it is properly developed, polished, and marketed.
